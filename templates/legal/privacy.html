What we collect

  • Account details you provide: username, optional display name, and a securely hashed password (we never store it in plain text).
  • Content you create: cards, images, articles, comments, and reports.
  • Operational logs: your IP address and basic request data, used for security, abuse prevention, and rate limiting.

Uploaded images are re-encoded on our server, which strips embedded metadata such as camera EXIF and GPS location before the image is stored.

How we use it

To run the site, show your content, keep accounts secure, and enforce our Acceptable Use Policy. We don't sell your personal information.

Sharing

We share data only with the service providers that host the site, and where required by law or to respond to illegal content (for example, referring CSAM to the authorities).

Your choices

You can request removal of your content or account by contacting us. Some records may be retained where we're legally required to keep them.

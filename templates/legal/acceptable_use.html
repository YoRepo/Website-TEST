Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: {{ now_year }}.

{{ SITE_NAME }} is a place to share original custom cards. To keep it usable for everyone, you agree not to upload, post, or link to any of the following.

Never allowed

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or any sexual content involving minors, real or drawn. This is reported to the authorities and the account is removed immediately.
  • Content that is illegal where you or we operate, or that promotes serious harm or violence against people.
  • Sexual content, gore, or shock imagery presented as card art.
  • Harassment, hate speech, or threats targeting people based on who they are.
  • Artwork or text you don't have the right to use (copyright/trademark infringement), including ripped official assets passed off as your own.
  • Malware, or attempts to break, overload, or probe the site's security.
  • Spam, scams, or bulk automated uploads.
  • Other people's private information.

How we enforce this

Anyone can report content. We may hide or delete content, hold uploads for review, and suspend or ban accounts. Uploaded images may be scanned automatically. Serious violations — especially CSAM — are referred to the appropriate authorities.
